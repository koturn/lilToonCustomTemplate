lilToonCustomTemplate
=====================

Templates for custom shader of [lilToon](https://github.com/lilxyzw/lilToon "lilxyzw/lilToon").

## Contents

- [Assets/koturn/Template](https://github.com/koturn/lilToonCustomTemplate/tree/main/Assets/koturn/Template "Assets/koturn/Template"): Original custom shader templates distributed on the [official website](https://lilxyzw.github.io/lilToon/ja_JP/dev/custom_shader.html "カスタムシェーダーの作り方 | lilToon").
- [Assets/koturn/TemplateAll](https://github.com/koturn/lilToonCustomTemplate/tree/main/Assets/koturn/TemplateAll "Assets/koturn/TemplateAll"): koturn's custom shader template.
- [Assets/koturn/lilToonGeometryFX](https://github.com/koturn/lilToonCustomTemplate/tree/main/Assets/koturn/lilToonGeometryFX "Assets/koturn/lilToonGeometryFX"): An example of a custom shader that utilizes the geometry shader distributed on the [official website](https://lilxyzw.github.io/lilToon/ja_JP/dev/custom_shader.html "カスタムシェーダーの作り方 | lilToon").
